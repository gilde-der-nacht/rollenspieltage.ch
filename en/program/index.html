Flea market

We would like to provide a flea market area this year. Every visitor of the Rollenspieltage is allowed to offer role-playing games and accessories for sale and can expand his own collection by buying books or other items from other attendees.

For simplicity’s sake we plan to run the flea market only during the breaks.
